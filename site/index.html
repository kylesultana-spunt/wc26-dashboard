Why trust these numbers

Most prediction sites hand you a number with no track record. We don't. The same model that prices these matches was run on three completed tournaments — 22,700 held-out market predictions — and the picks at our 72%+ bet rule landed 81.2% of the time. Probabilities are Platt-calibrated per market family and capped where the model was shown to be overconfident.

Goals use an Elo-driven Dixon-Coles curve; corners, shots and fouls a negative-binomial with opponent and Elo-gap adjustment; cards are referee-adjusted (blending each official's international and ~88k club matches); player props are per-90 with shrinkage. 12,000 Monte-Carlo simulations per fixture.

PITCHIQ — predictions from a Monte-Carlo model (Elo + Dixon-Coles goals, negative-binomial corners/shots/fouls, referee-adjusted cards, per-90 player props), Platt-calibrated per market family and validated on Euro 2024, Copa América 2024 & WC 2022. Odds shown are the model's fair price, not a bookmaker offer.
